Brief History

The Begininng Of The Journey

The Journey of Redeemed Christian Fellowship Senior Friends' Association(RCFSFA) started during 2004 edition of the Annual National Leadership Training Programme(LTP). Three people were called out and prayed for by Pastor E.A Odeyemi(Vice President, CRM-Admin/Evangelism) and Pastor J.F(S.A to G.O Admin/Personnel, RCCG). They are:
1. Pastor Dele Balogun-President
2. Pastor Steve Friday- General Secretary
3. Pastor Sesan Akinawo- Pro

Since the inauguration, the activities has been mainly mobilization and creating awareness . All the previous meetings held were always during the special programmes and with that, there is always little or no room for proper and comprehensive deliberations(Due to the fact that people are always in a hurry to leave after the Holy Ghost Service).

The Executive Arm

Due to the enormity of the challenges of the Association, the executive arm was expanded. On Saturday 6th March,2010, The New set of expanded executive was prayed for by Pastor E.A Odeyemi(Vice President, CRM-Admin/Evangelism) at RCCG Recharge Centre (now Amazing Grace Assembly), Redemption Camp

Our Source

RCFSFA is an off-shot of Christ The Redeemer's Ministries (CRM), a conglomeration of many ministries which was founded on April 7,1977 as a protest against the revival of idolatry in our land-when Nigeria played host to the second World Black Festival of Arts and Culture(FESTAC). The General Overseer of the RCCG (Pastor E.A Adeboye) is the President of CRM under which RCFSFA operates.

Membership

The Membership of this Association is drawn thus:
(a) those who fellowshipped at RCF while in campus.
(a) those who fellowshipped at RCCF during NYSC year.
(c) those currently fellowshipping at RCSF at the various campuses.
(d)the Spouse of a,b or/and c above.
(e) friends of the Association.
